About the Institution

Ramakrishna Mission Vidyamandira is a premier residential autonomous college under the University of Calcutta, run by the Ramakrishna Mission. It stands for academic excellence blended with spiritual and cultural values. Our aim is to form a community of scholars dedicated to knowledge, service, and character building.
